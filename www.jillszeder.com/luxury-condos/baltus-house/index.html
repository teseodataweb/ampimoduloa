    Baltus House

    Description
    Amenities
    Residence Features

    Miami's Design District is full of creativity and the skyline only gets more impressive once you gaze upon the stylish Baltus House condo by South Florida's Related Group. This fifteen story building features 167 Miami condos for sale near many popular stores and is just a couple of feet away from the cerulean waters of the always active Biscayne Bay.

    Yoga, Pilates, a massive 55 foot long swimming pool, a rooftop terrace boasting a Jacuzzi, gorgeous artwork by Jorge Perez throughout the lobbies and common areas, Baltus House upholds the strict attention to form, function, aesthetic, style and much more. The Miami condos for sale of Baltus House have 520 to 1,720 square feet and up to three bedrooms. Expect to see European cabinetry, porcelain tile, terraces with captivating vistas, and a host of additional features that further elevate the appeal of Baltus House.

    The Miami Design District has many places to explore and this increases immensely when you venture beyond and visit the downtown area of Miami which includes many large scale attractions including the home of the Miami Heat, better known as the American Airlines Arena. To the east of Baltus House across the water is Miami Beach.

    • Professional-grade cardio machines
    • Weight-training area
    • Yoga and Pilates area
    • Direct access to pool deck
    • 55-foot-long pool overlooking Biscayne Bay and Miami Beach
    • 400 sqft. reflected pool
    • Oversized whirlpool
    • Plush chaises
    • Private cabanas
    • Outdoor club seating featuring sofas, chairs, ottomans and more.

    • Professional-grade stainless steel appliance package with refrigerator/freezer, ceramic glass cook top, built-in oven, low noise dishwasher and built-in microwave
    • Double-basin under mounted sinks
    • Designer faucets with pull-out sprayers
    • Imported porcelain tile flooring in the bathrooms and kitchen
